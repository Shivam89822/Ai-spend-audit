# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Product Overview

AI Spend Audit Platform is a lightweight SaaS tool designed to help individuals, startups, and small engineering teams analyze and optimize their AI software spending.

The platform audits subscriptions across tools such as:

* ChatGPT
* Cursor
* Claude
* GitHub Copilot
* Gemini
* Windsurf

and generates:

* cost optimization recommendations
* downgrade opportunities
* annual savings estimates
* shareable audit reports

---

# Target Users

## Primary Audience

### 1. Small Startup Teams

Teams using multiple AI tools without clear visibility into total monthly spend.

### 2. Indie Hackers & Freelancers

Developers and creators paying for overlapping AI subscriptions individually.

### 3. Engineering Teams

Companies experimenting with AI tooling but lacking centralized cost optimization.

---

# Core Problem

AI subscriptions are becoming fragmented and difficult to track.

Most users:

* subscribe impulsively
* keep overlapping tools active
* upgrade to team plans too early
* underestimate annual AI spending

The platform solves this by:

* centralizing AI spend visibility
* identifying unnecessary upgrades
* generating actionable optimization suggestions

---

# Product Positioning

The product is positioned as:

* an AI SaaS optimization tool
* a lightweight financial audit assistant
* a developer-focused spend intelligence platform

The focus is:

* clarity
* actionable recommendations
* fast onboarding
* shareable insights

rather than deep enterprise analytics.
